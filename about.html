Our Organization

  Ross Conspiracies Inc. is a site where a few trusted authors have got together and decided to voice our opinions on a few things. Mainly about conspiracies such as Squirrels are really government spies, the Earth is flat, and other various things.

  Usually, when people hear conspiracy, they think about a bunch of whackjobs that are paranoid about getting sucked up in an extra-terrestrial’s spaceship (this is actually a legitimate concern), and then the aliens bring the guy back down to Earth and promptly leave with no trace of ever being there. That isn’t what we are about. Instead of telling everyone about the spaceship and letting the public demoralize us, we find enough clues that we really did get sucked up into the spaceship, and the aliens were real. Generally the public says “low resolution pictures of the sky in black and white aren’t proving your theory”, but what do they know? The aliens didn’t pick them up.

  We not only have conspiracy theories, but we also have a lot of ideas on what you can do to prevent the government from reading your mind, how to cure yourself from wrinkle nose syndrome (carried by plutians, and makes you look permanently disgusted), how broccoli wasn’t ever meant to be eaten, and so on and so on.

  A few of our better known articles are “8 Ways Goats Affect Your Health; You Won’t Believe Number 5!”, “Seriously Though, Why are the Aliens Doing This?”, and “Mircowaves: Brain Fryers”. A few of our best authors are Luke Groundwalker, Brian Flyers, Wally Mart, and Jane.
